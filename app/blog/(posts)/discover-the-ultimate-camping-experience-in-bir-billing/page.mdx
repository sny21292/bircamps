# Discover the ultimate camping experience in Bir Billing

<img className="post-hero" src="/photos/Discover-the-Ultimate-Camping-Experience-in-Bir-Billing.png" alt="The ultimate camping experience in Bir Billing" />

<p className="lead">Tucked into the foothills of the Dhauladhar range, Bir Billing has quietly become one of the most loved getaways in North India. Here's what makes a night here different.</p>

## A setting that does the work for you

Our camp sits in **Gunehr village**, two kilometres from Bir, ringed by green mountains with a cold, clear river a five-minute walk away. You don't have to go looking for the view — it's there the moment you step out of your tent, from sunrise mist to the last gold light on the ridges.

## The things you'll remember

- **The bonfire.** Every night ends around the fire, with chai, food, and a circle of people who arrived as strangers.
- **The river.** A short walk to glacier-fed water — bracing, beautiful and quiet.
- **The sky.** Paragliders by day, an astonishing spread of stars by night.
- **The welcome.** Small, family-run, and genuinely glad you came.

## Comfort, your way

"Camping" here can mean whatever you want it to. Rough it in a dome tent, keep it easy in a bell tent with meals included, or treat yourself to a luxury Swiss tent or our new wooden cottage. The view is the same from every one.

## Away from the chaos

Maybe the best thing about Bir is what it *doesn't* have: traffic, noise, crowds, hurry. Guests tell us again and again that the real luxury is switching off — putting the phone down, watching the valley, and doing very little for a day or two.

> "An exceptionally superb view and staff that makes you feel like family — a true WOW experience, far from the chaos of the city."

That's the camping experience we try to give everyone who comes up the mountain. Come see for yourself — [browse the camps](/stay) and pick your night.
