# Bir Billing camping packages: how to plan the perfect weekend

<img className="post-hero" src="/photos/Bir-Billing-Camping-Packages.webp" alt="A camping package weekend in Bir Billing" />

<p className="lead">Two days, one night, and a sky full of paragliders. A weekend is just enough time to taste the best of Bir Billing without rushing. Here's how we'd plan it.</p>

## What a good package includes

The building blocks of a great Bir weekend are simple: a comfortable place to sleep, a bonfire dinner, a paragliding flight, and a little time to explore. A typical package brings these together so you arrive and just enjoy yourself:

- One night's stay in the tent or cottage of your choice
- Dinner, bonfire and breakfast
- A tandem paragliding flight (Classic or Prime)
- Help arranging a scooty or bike if you want to roam

## A sample 2-day itinerary

### Day 1
- **Afternoon** — Arrive, check in, settle into camp. Walk down to the river.
- **Evening** — Tea on the deck as the valley turns gold. Dinner at the bonfire.
- **Night** — Stargazing, then a warm tent.

### Day 2
- **Early morning** — Drive up to Billing for a paragliding flight while the air is calm.
- **Late morning** — Land at Bir, breakfast back at camp.
- **Afternoon** — A short trek to the waterfalls or a ride toward Rajgundha before you head home.

## How much should you budget?

It depends on the tent and the flight you choose. As a rough guide per person:

- **Stay:** ₹850–₹2,000
- **Paragliding:** ₹3,000 (Classic) or ₹4,500 (Prime)
- **Extras:** vehicle rental, café stops and snacks

> The trick to a perfect weekend is leaving room in it. Pick one flight, one walk, and one long evening by the fire — and let the rest happen.
